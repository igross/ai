# Bookmaster Plan

## Purpose
- Master outline and structure for the book.

## Target Audience (Working Definition)
- Intelligent general readers (UK/US), including people who read narrative non-fiction, long-form journalism, and policy-adjacent books.
- Primary reader profile: interested in how rules, incentives, and measurement shape behaviour, but without formal economics training.
- Secondary reader profile: practitioners in policy, education, health, business, and media who want usable intuition rather than technical proofs.
- Reading-level target: plain-English argument with optional technical depth only where it materially improves understanding.
- Promise to reader: by the end of the book, readers can spot target-trap dynamics in everyday systems and ask better design questions.
