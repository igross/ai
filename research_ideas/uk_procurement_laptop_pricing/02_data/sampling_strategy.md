# sampling strategy

## phase 1 credible sample
Target contracts with:
- explicit unit price and quantity in attachments,
- single-SKU laptop line items,
- clear award dates and buyer identity,
- sufficient SKU detail for high-confidence matching.

Purpose: maximise measurement credibility before expanding coverage.

## phase 2 broader sample
Add:
- multi-SKU contracts with line-item details,
- broader buyer coverage,
- lower-confidence matches for appendix analyses.

## exclusion rules
Exclude contracts where laptops are incidental to broader managed-service procurement and hardware price cannot be isolated.

## representativeness checks
- Compare buyer-type mix and route mix between phase 1 and phase 2.
- Report whether baseline findings are sensitive to sample broadening.
