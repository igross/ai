# heterogeneity plan

## buyer type
Estimate separate effects for:
- NHS,
- local authorities,
- central government,
- other public bodies.

## region
Use region fixed effects and interaction terms as a proxy for delivery/logistics complexity.

## contract size
Assess whether scale (quantity bins or log quantity interactions) attenuates or amplifies price gaps.

## framework lot and supplier concentration
Where lot/supplier data allow, test whether concentration within framework segments predicts larger gaps.

## interpretation guardrails
- Heterogeneity estimates are exploratory unless pre-specified and adequately powered.
- Always report subgroup sample sizes and overlap diagnostics.
