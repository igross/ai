# falsification tests

## placebo category test
If feasible, build a matched sample for highly commoditised accessories where large persistent overpayment is less expected after comparability alignment. Significant gaps there would indicate residual measurement or benchmark issues.

## bundle-mechanism falsification
If bundling fully explains observed laptop gaps, then in hardware-only line items with matched warranties the average gap should be close to zero.

## timing placebo
Use deliberately misaligned benchmark dates (e.g., far from award date) to verify that estimated gaps worsen as expected; this checks sensitivity to temporal mismatch logic.

## route placebo (where feasible)
Within narrowly defined high-competition open procedures, direct-award effects should not appear by construction.

## implementation notes
- Treat falsification failures as design diagnostics, not appendix-only footnotes.
- Revisit matching and bundle flags before interpreting mechanism coefficients.
