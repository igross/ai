# discussion and policy notes

## interpretation framework
- A positive observed gap is not automatically waste.
- First decompose into hardware-equivalent vs bundled/non-separable components.

## policy levers by mechanism
- Bundling channel: improve line-item disclosure and standardized service coding.
- Competition channel: governance around direct awards and mini-competition design.
- Timing channel: reduce cycle lags and improve benchmark-refresh protocols.

## external validity and limits
- Results pertain to standardized IT goods, not necessarily complex bespoke procurement.
- Measurement depends on attachment availability and parse quality.
