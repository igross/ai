# research question

Do UK public-sector buyers pay higher per-unit prices for comparable laptop SKUs than contemporaneous market benchmarks, after harmonising VAT treatment, warranty/service content, and procurement mechanism?

## unit of analysis
Primary unit: `(sku i, contract j, award date t, buyer b)`.

## estimands
- Average log price gap for comparable hardware-only SKUs.
- Conditional price-gap effects by procurement route and effective competition.
- Bundling-adjusted decomposition of observed overpayment.
