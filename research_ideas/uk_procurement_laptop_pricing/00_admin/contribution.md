# contribution

## measurement contribution
Build a line-item dataset linking UK laptop procurement unit prices to contemporaneous market benchmarks at SKU-date level with explicit comparability flags.

## mechanism contribution
Separate three channels usually conflated in public-vs-private comparisons:
1. bundled service content,
2. competition intensity/procurement route,
3. governance/timing frictions.

## policy contribution
Provide evidence on where apparent overpayment is real versus compensation for service/compliance bundles, informing framework design and buyer guidance.
