# data sources (UK-feasible)

## price outcomes
1. ONS CPI by COICOP category.
2. ONS CPIH and services vs goods inflation series.
3. ONS regional inflation measures (including any experimental CPIH regional outputs, if available).

## expenditure weights and demographic demand
4. ONS Living Costs and Food Survey (LCF): expenditure shares by age and household type.
5. Supplemental household budget sources if needed for category crosswalks.

## pension policy and income series
6. DWP/ONS statistics on pensioner incomes and state pension amounts.
7. Official history of state pension uprating/triple-lock implementation.
8. Components used in uprating formula (earnings growth, CPI inflation, floor component).

## regional exposure
9. ONS population by age (LA/NUTS/region) to construct retiree share.
10. Potential pension-income-share proxies by region (subject to availability).

## supply-side proxies
11. Business counts/employment for gardening, horticulture, local leisure, and personal services.
12. Labour intensity proxies by sector from UK input-output or business surveys.
13. Local capacity constraints: vacancies, wage pressure, or business density indicators.

## policy and macro controls
14. National macro controls (energy/food shocks, broad wage growth).
15. Policy series relevant to financing instruments (including NIC changes where applicable).

## data assembly notes
- Do not assume direct micro-level access until licensing and download steps are confirmed.
- Maintain a source manifest with fields: dataset, frequency, geography, variable IDs, and licensing constraints.
