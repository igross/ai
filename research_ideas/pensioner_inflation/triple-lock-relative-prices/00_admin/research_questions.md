# research questions

## main question
Triple lock -> sectoral relative prices?

## sub-questions
1. Is the main channel demand composition? Under what conditions (inelastic supply, non-tradables, retiree concentration)?
2. Is there an additional marginal-cost channel through financing instruments (e.g., employer NIC)?
3. Are effects regional (retiree-heavy areas) and category-specific (services vs tradables)?
4. What is the welfare/distribution implication (workers vs pensioners; renters vs owners)?

## scope note
This project focuses on UK institutions and data first, with external evidence used only to sharpen mechanism tests.
