# contribution

## planned contributions
1. Mechanism contribution: integrate transfer indexation, age-specific demand composition, and non-tradable supply elasticity in one minimal framework.
2. Measurement contribution: construct a pensioner-weighted inflation metric and connect it to category-level inflation outcomes.
3. Empirical contribution: implement UK exposure-based design that uses national policy shocks with regional heterogeneity.

## boundaries
- The project does not claim aggregate inflation determination.
- The project targets relative inflation in exposed categories and locations.
- Any welfare claims will remain conditional on estimated magnitudes.
