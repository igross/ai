# results plan

## table plan
1. Descriptive statistics: exposure, shocks, and category inflation moments.
2. Baseline shift-share estimates.
3. Heterogeneity by supply elasticity proxy.
4. Cost-channel extension with labour intensity/payroll proxy.
5. Robustness: alternative shocks, exposures, and baskets.

## figure plan
1. Pension uprating vs CPI/earnings timeline.
2. Pensioner vs headline inflation wedge over time.
3. Event-study style coefficients around policy episodes.
4. Coefficient heterogeneity by retiree exposure bins.

## interpretation guardrails
- Report effect sizes as relative-price changes, not broad inflation claims.
- Distinguish reduced-form correlation from identified mechanisms unless assumptions hold.
- Highlight null and mixed results explicitly.
